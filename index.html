10+ Years of Craft

Our Story

Born From a Love
of Coffee

Morning Brew began in 2015 as a dream in a tiny apartment kitchen. Our founder, Elena Rosario, spent years traveling through Colombia, Ethiopia, and Japan—falling deeper in love with the art of coffee at every stop.

She returned with a mission: bring world-class, thoughtfully sourced coffee to a neighborhood setting where everyone feels at home. No pretension. Just craft, community, and really good coffee.

Today, we partner directly with six small-batch farms across three continents, roast in-house every Monday, and train our baristas for 200+ hours before they pull their first shot for a guest.
